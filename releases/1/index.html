Welcome to the Puyo Puyo Chain Simulator. This chain simulator will allow you construct chains, similar to Puyo Puyo, and test them. This chain simulator also contains a large amount of fever chains, from Puyo Puyo Fever and Puyo Puyo! 15th Anniversary.

There are 2 versions of this Puyo Puyo Chain Simulator:

Standard Mode: Standard Mode is designed to be simple yet look more improved then the versions this chain simulator is based off of.
Eye Candy Mode: Eye Candy Mode is designed for people who have a fast connection and want to have a lot of pretty & colorful graphics. This mode features colored backgrounds similar to fever mode in Puyo Puyo Fever.
